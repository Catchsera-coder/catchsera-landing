FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you want to know before getting started.

How does Catchsera work exactly?+
A customer calls your restaurant's phone number. Catchsera's AI (Sera) answers the call, takes the full order in natural voice conversation, confirms it with the customer, and instantly sends the order to your dashboard. Your team sees it in real time and starts preparing.
Do I need special hardware or equipment?+
No. Catchsera works with your existing phone number. Your staff views orders on any device with a browser — tablet, phone, laptop, or desktop PC. For receipt printing, any USB or WiFi thermal printer works.
What languages does Sera speak?+
Sera currently supports English only. Spanish is available as a paid add-on. More languages are on the roadmap.
What if a customer has a complex or unusual order?+
Sera is trained on your specific menu and handles modifications, substitutions, allergies, and extras. For truly complex situations, she can gracefully hand off to a human staff member if you configure that option.
How long does setup take?+
Most restaurants are live in under 10 minutes. You provide your menu, phone number, and business hours. We handle the AI training and phone integration. No IT team needed.
Can I try it for free?+
Yes! Every new account gets a 7-day free trial with full access to all Growth plan features. Book a free demo and our team will get you set up within 24 hours. Cancel any time — no questions asked.
Does it work with delivery apps like DoorDash or Uber Eats?+
Catchsera handles direct phone orders. It works alongside delivery apps — many restaurants use both. Pro plan customers can request custom integrations with their existing POS or delivery platforms.
Is my data secure?+
Absolutely. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We use enterprise-grade cloud infrastructure. Your customer data is never sold or shared with third parties.
